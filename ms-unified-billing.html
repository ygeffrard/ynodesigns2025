MS Unified Billing

We built an easy-to-use app for Morgan Stanley to help them with their billing process. It replaced their old, complicated system and reduced mistakes, making their work faster and more efficient

Accomplishments

  • Cut billing time from 9 minutes to 2 minutes
  • Developed a unified data view for all teams
  • Boosted response times with live collaboration
  • Added intelligent technologies for greater efficiency

Problem

The client and institutional billing teams work inefficiently because their outdated systems don’t communicate in real time, and many tasks are done manually in Excel

Strategy

Transform decades of complexity into a new, streamlined experience by identifying key pain points through research, mapping personas of all user groups, analyzing current information architecture, and pinpointing essential data points.

Workshop

Creating a common view with shared columns in the main search result table helped all user groups work together more efficiently and collaborate better

AI smart alert

Using AI to spot when past billing changes don’t follow the rules (SEC or Morgan Stanley) and alerting us when something is wrong

How we worked

We worked closely with the product and development teams to define a clear plan for this project, creating a roadmap for success. We ensured the application met WCAG 2.1 AA standards and followed the client’s design system, delivering an accessible and cohesive experience
